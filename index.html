Background

Final year undergraduate researcher at PES University, pursuing Electronics and Communication Engineering with specialization in VLSI and Quantum Technology. Research focus encompasses quantum computing architectures, computer architecture optimization, and electronic design automation frameworks.

Active Research Projects

Quantum Computing Architectures: Collaborating with Prof. Sumit K. Mandal at CSA, IISc on distributed quantum computing architectures and quantum error correction protocols for near-term quantum devices.

Combinatorics on Words: Previous research collaboration with Prof. Sivasankar M focusing on enumeration and localization algorithms for factors and subwords in combinatorial structures.

Photonic Quantum Computing: Current undergraduate thesis project developing a Photonic FPGA framework for Variational Quantum Algorithms, conducted in the Photonics and Quantum Tech Lab under supervision of Prof. Kaustav Bhowmick.
