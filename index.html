Research

My research centers on quantum computing, theoretical computer science, and computer architecture. Currently, I am focused on building fully-quantum communication systems for multicore quantum processors at IISc Bangalore.

Previous work includes quantum circuit design for applications in combinatorics on words, with Prof. M Sivasankar. I am also exploring quantum neural networks and variational algorithms as part of my ongoing research at PES University.
