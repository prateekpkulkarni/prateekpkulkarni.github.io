About

I'm a 3rd year undergraduate student at PES University, pursuing a degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering with a specialization in VLSI and Quantum Technology. My research interests center around the capabilities and limits of quantum computers, and computational complexity theory more generally.

Research

Currently, I am working in the Future Computing Systems (FIST) Lab with Prof. Sumit K Mandal at CSA, IISc. My current research focuses on:

  • Quantum query complexity of Boolean functions
  • Architectures for distributed quantum computing systems
  • Quantum error correction for near-term quantum devices

Previously, I have worked with Prof. Sivasankar M on some problems in combinatorics on words (enumerating/locating factors/subwords).
