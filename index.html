about

I am a final year undergraduate pursuing BTech in Electronics and Communication Engineering, with specialization in VLSI and Quantum Technology at PES University. Research interests span quantum computing architectures, advanced memory systems, and electronic design automation for next-generation computing platforms.

research

I work on quantum computing systems and memory architectures. Currently collaborating with Prof. Sumit K. Mandal at CSA, IISc on distributed quantum computing architectures and error correction for near-term devices. My undergraduate thesis with Prof. Kaustav Bhowmick explores variational quantum algorithms. I am also very keen on developing advanced memory systems using emerging technologies like 3D XPoint and memristors, with intelligent in-DRAM prefetching techniques.
