About

I'm a final year undergraduate student at PES University, pursuing a degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering with a specialization in VLSI and Quantum Technology.

My research lies at the intersection of quantum computing and computer systems. I'm particularly interested in understanding the fundamental limits of quantum computation and developing practical architectures for near-term quantum devices.

Research

I work with Prof. Sumit K. Mandal at CSA, IISc on architectures for distributed quantum computing, quantum complexity theory and quantum error correction for near-term devices more recently.

Previously, I worked with Prof. Sivasankar M on combinatorics on words, specifically on problems related to enumerating and locating factors and subwords in Thue-Morse and Fibonacci words.
