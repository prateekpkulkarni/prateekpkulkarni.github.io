I research the capabilities and limits of quantum computers. Currently, I am a Predoctoral Fellow at the Department of Computer Science and Automation, IISc.

My work focuses on the intersection of theoretical computer science, computer architecture and quantum computing, specifically designing coupling maps and error correction schemes for quantum processors, with theoretical guarantees.
