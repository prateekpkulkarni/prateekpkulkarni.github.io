About

I am a 3rd year undergraduate student at PES University, pursuing a degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering with a specialization in VLSI and Quantum Technology.

My research interests center around theoretical aspects of quantum computing, particularly in quantum complexity theory and the fundamental limits of quantum computers. I am also interested in combinatorial problems related to theoretical computer science.

Research

I am currently working in the Future Computing Systems (FIST) Lab with Prof. Sumit K Mandal at Computer Science and Automation, Indian Institute of Science. My work focuses on topics in quantum complexity theory, distributed quantum computing and quantum error correction.

Previously, I have worked with Prof. Sivasankar M on problems in combinatorics on words, specifically on enumerating and locating factors/subwords in formal languages.
