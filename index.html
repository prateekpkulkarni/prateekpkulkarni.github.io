\section{introduction}

I study the theoretical limits of quantum architecture.

I am a Predoctoral Fellow at the Department of Computer Science and Automation (CSA), IISc.

My research sits at the boundary of theory and quantum hardware. Currently, I am interested in designing coupling maps (graphs representing qubit connectivity) that are sparse enough to be built, but connected enough to run error correction efficiently.
