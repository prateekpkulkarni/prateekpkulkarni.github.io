about

I'm a final year undergraduate student at PES University, pursuing a degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering with a specialization in VLSI and Quantum Technology. My research interests are in quantum complexity theory, distributed quantum computing, and quantum error correction.

research

I work with Prof. Sumit K. Mandal at CSA, IISc on architectures for distributed quantum computing and quantum error correction for near-term devices.

Previously, I have worked with Prof. Sivasankar M on combinatorics on words (enumerating/locating factors and subwords).

For my undergraduate thesis, I am working on building a Photonic FPGA for Variational Quantum Algorithms, in the Photonics and Quantum Tech Lab, advised by Prof. Kaustav Bhowmick.
