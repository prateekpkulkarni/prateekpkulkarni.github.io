I am a final year undergraduate pursuing BTech in Electronics and Communication Engineering, with specialization in VLSI and Quantum Technology at PES University, focused on quantum computing architectures, advanced memory systems, and electronic design automation. My interests lie in taking advantage of the structure found in quantum error models to improve reliability in quantum computers. I have also done work on near term quantum algorithms, memory systems optimization through intelligent prefetching, and development of advanced memory architectures.

I grew up in Karnataka and received my Bachelor's degree from PES University in 2025. I then worked with Prof. Sumit K. Mandal at the Future Computing Systems (FIST) Lab at CSA, IISc on distributed quantum computing architectures and error correction for near-term devices.
