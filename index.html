About

I am a final year undergraduate pursuing BTech in Electronics and Communication Engineering, with specialization in VLSI and Quantum Technology at PES University. My research interests span quantum computing architectures and electronic design automation for next-generation computing platforms.

Currently, I am working with Prof. Sumit K. Mandal in the Future Computing Systems (FIST) Lab at CSA, IISc on distributed quantum computing architectures and error correction for near-term devices. This work is supported by the Walmart Center for Tech Excellence.

My undergraduate thesis with Prof. Kaustav Bhowmick explores variational quantum algorithms.

I am deeply interested in developing advanced memory systems, particularly with emerging technologies such as 3D XPoint and memristors, as well as intelligent in-DRAM prefetching. More recently, I have also been drawn to ML4Systems, with a special focus on LLM-aided design.
