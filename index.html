Prateek P. Kulkarni

I am a final-year undergraduate student in Electronics & Communication Engineering at PES University, specializing in VLSI and Quantum Technology. My research interests center on the capabilities and limits of quantum computers, with current work focusing on distributed quantum systems and error correction at the Future Computing Systems Lab (FIST) Lab, IISc. I am also interested in computer architecture and electronic design automation for next-generation computing platforms.
