Research Interests

Quantum Computing

I work with Prof. Sumit K. Mandal in the Future Computing Systems (FIST) Lab, at CSA, IISc on architectures for distributed quantum computing and quantum error correction for near-term devices.

VLSI Design & Computer Architecture

Developing novel memory architectures and in-DRAM prefetching techniques to improve system performance. Research includes 3D XPoint and memristor technologies.

Quantum Machine Learning

For my undergraduate thesis, I am working on building a Photonic FPGA for Variational Quantum Algorithms in the Photonics and Quantum Tech Lab, advised by Prof. Kaustav Bhowmick.
