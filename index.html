I am a Predoctoral Fellow (SPARKS) in the Department of Computer Science and Automation at the Indian Institute of Science, advised by Prof. Sumit K. Mandal. I am concurrently pursuing a B.Tech. in ECE (Quantum Technology track) at PES University (Class of 2026).

My research centers on quantum complexity theory and the algorithmic aspects of near-term quantum computation. At IISc, I use tools from theoretical computer science to analyze and inform better quantum hardware design, particularly around qubit connectivity and circuit compilation. Our work on coupling map design received the ACM SRC Bronze Medal at MICRO 2025 (UG category).
