Prateek P. Kulkarni

Pre-Doctoral Fellow, CSA, IISc


I am a predoctoral fellow at IISc under the SPARKS Programme, advised by Prof. Sumit K. Mandal. I am concurrently completing a B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering (Quantum Technology track) at PES University (Class of 2026).


My work sits at the intersection of theoretical computer science and quantum hardware. I am interested in building reliable and efficient quantum computing systems — understanding how constraints across abstraction layers limit real devices, and closing that gap to make quantum advantage practically realizable. My recent focus has been on designing better coupling maps and optimal resource allocation strategies for noisy quantum devices.


If you think our interests overlap, I’d love to hear from you.
