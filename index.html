Prateek P Kulkarni

I am a 3rd year engineering student at PES University majoring in Electronics, specializing in VLSI with a specific inclination towards quantum technology. I like designing architectures for quantum computers!

Current Positions

Visiting Research Student at the Future Computing Systems Lab, Dept. of Computer Science and Automation, Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore under Prof. Sumit Kumar Mandal, exploring the aspects of quantum interconnects and quantum multicore architecture.

Long-Term Research Intern at the Center for Information Security, Forensics and Cyber Resilience (ISFCR), PES University. Part of the Applied Cryptography and Blockchain Lab, led by Prof. Indu Radhakrishnan, working on quantum/post-quantum cryptography.

Research Assistant in the Photonics & Quantum Technology Lab, PES University under Prof. Kaustav Bhowmick, working on quantum machine learning and possible applications elsewhere.

Research Focus

I'm heavily interested in all aspects of quantum computing, theoretical computer science, and computer architecture. Lately, I have also been invested in quantum cryptography. However, my attention span often lasts so long that it is apt to say my hop count tends to infinity! Usually, my research spans two or multiple of these areas.

Currently, I am working on the architecture of multicore quantum computers, for systems employing superconducting qubits @FIST Lab, CSA - IISc.

Additionally, starting to work on Combinatorics on Words with Prof. Sivasankar M.
