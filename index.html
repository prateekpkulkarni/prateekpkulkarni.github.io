Research

My research centers on quantum computing, theoretical computer science, and computer architecture. Currently, I am focused on building fully-quantum communication systems for multicore quantum processors at IISc Bangalore.

Previous work includes quantum circuit design for applications in combinatorics on words, with Prof. M Sivasankar. I am exploring quantum neural networks and variational algorithms as part of my ongoing research at PES University.

I am also starting to work on various aspects of computer architecture security and emerging memory technologies at the Center for Hardware Acceleration and Chip Security (cHACS), advised by Prof. Sudeendra Kumar K.
